The Company

XTENSION, our retail outlet started in December 2012 for regular and PLUS size women who like stylish, trendy and comfortable clothing.

While our retail space is fairly new, we have been in the garment industry for the last 20 years and have done work for quite a few fashion houses in Europe and the Gulf. We have also done work for large well known retail outlets in India.
